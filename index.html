About Me

Accessibility Testing & Consulting Professional | 3.6+ Years of Experience

I am an Accessibility Testing & Consulting Professional with 3.6+ years of experience, specialized in Web & Mobile (iOS/Android) accessibility testing. I have strong working knowledge of WCAG 2.0, 2.1, 2.2, ADA, Section 508, AODA, and global accessibility compliance standards.

I am proficient in semantic HTML, WAI-ARIA implementation, accessibility heuristics, and VPAT creation. I use a wide range of assistive technologies—including NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, and TalkBack—to identify accessibility gaps, usability barriers, and compliance issues across digital products.

My strengths include providing clear remediation guidance, partnering effectively with development teams, validating fixes, reviewing accessibility bugs submitted by other testers, and ensuring full accessibility throughout the product lifecycle.

I am detail-oriented, collaborative, and highly user-focused, with a strong passion for building inclusive and barrier-free digital experiences for people with disabilities. I stay continuously updated on accessibility laws, WCAG updates, and industry best practices to help teams deliver fully accessible and compliant solutions.
